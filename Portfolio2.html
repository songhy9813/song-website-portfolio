Kids art class

Overview

These drawings are examples to show to students when I worked as an art teacher. Students feel difficult about the topic, but through my example, they understand what they should draw.

Content

These drawings consist of two objects and one action. The drawing subject that I thought are as followed:
• Use stones and walnuts to express destruction.
• Use dragonflies and ice to express confinement.
• Use knitting and plastic gloves to express how to make something.
