Reasearch for Design - User Trialling

Overview

This research journal a group assignment for the second part of the Research in Design course of the first semester. The aim of this research is to evaluate the user experience of the Rijksstudio digital environment. The importance of this work is, for us to come up together as a group with a research plan for user trialling for the Rijksstudio website.

Content

There are research protocaol and interview. The Research questions that we aimed to answer with this structure are as followed:
• What do young adults, who are interested in art actually do during user trialling when using Rijksstudio?
• What usability problems are users facing when using Rijksstudio? What causes these problems?
• How do the website’s layout and UI elements help a user to follow its flow and support what a user does?
